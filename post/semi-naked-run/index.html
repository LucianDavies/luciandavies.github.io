Semi naked run

June 15, 2020

This story unfolds in form 2 means I was around 14 years old and the day started out as a drag with some double of period of a subject I don’t clearly recall. We had physical education for the next 2 periods and because of summer we had swimming next. The whole class all approximately twenty five boys with heavy bags filled with books began the long track to the swimming pool area right at the other end of the large farm sized school.

The physical teacher called Mr Gumnyu a fit man most probably in his 40s then was our teacher for this semester. His character was best summarised by his strong love for sports, corporal punishment and very tight tiny sports shorts. On this day we met him going the opposite direction of the pool towards the school administration block. With a thinly veiled threat he said he wanted to see us already at the pool ready to start when he gets there.

Two thirds of the way there when the stories of the weekend and laughter filled the air when we noticed on the other side of of the rugby field Mr Gumunyu bolting towards the swimming pool gates. Panic erupted cause everyone knew what laid ahead for those that arrive after him. So we tried to outrun our fate. My group was half running half laughing at the ridiculousness of the situation. Its safe to say we got lined up and received two lashes with a recently broken piece of stick fashioned into a cane for the purpose of making a point.

We all changed and did our lapse enjoying the cold water on a summer day when I realized that I had forgot about the bell. Ringing this bell and in the case of our school it was closer to a air raid warning system you now only hear in old war movies but maybe not as loud. This system basically ran the school schedule for students signalling when classes ended when break time start when classes start again and most importantly the end of school. My class was the one responsible for this and today was my turn to sound it and I had forgotten.

With no time to waste I wrapped a towel and broke out into a stride. My mission was to sound alarm before the deputy headmaster noticed because that would only result in one thing. I was starting to pant only having made halfway through the fields and now becoming more and more aware that just wrapping a towel may not have been the best ideas. More and more students more visible as I made my way towards the Beit hall with was right next to not only the main offices the quadrangles that housed most of the classes at the school. People started to notice the fear of being caught made my hear beat loud in my chest but I kept pushing forward. Made it around the back the hall and flipped the switch. At that moment everyones head span around as is the case when you hear a loud noise and he I was having to wait for a full on minute glancing around at the eyes looking at me with confusion.

It then happened this lady teacher screamed at me in Shona.

Iweee. Why usina kupfeka?

Which translates hey why aren’t you dressed? I was mortified and in response I unwrapped my towel and showed her my tiny purple swimming trunks screaming back.

Ndakapfeka!

Which mean I am dressed. She was mortified and in hindsight it didn’t make sense but I was not waiting around to find out. I flipped the switch and bolted back to the swimming. Ignoring any calls in my general direction. We did get another 3 lashes from Mr Gumunyu after having cause us playing on the water polo goal frames instead of doing laps but the rest day was not eventful. Lady luck was with me on that day
