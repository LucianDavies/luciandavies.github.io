Semi naked run

June 15, 2020

This story unfolds in form 2 means I was around 14 years old and the day started out as a drag with some double of period of a subject i dont clearly recall. We had physical education for the next 2 periods amd because of summer we had swimming next. The whole class all 20something boys with heavy bags filled with books began the long track to the swimming pool area right at the other end of our 22+ hactare school.

The physical teacher called Mr Gumnyu a fit man most probably in his 40s then was our teacher for this semester. His character was best summarised by his strong love for sports, corporal punishment and very tight tiny sports shorts. On this day we met him going the opposite direction of the pool towards the school administration block. With a thinly veiled threat he said he wanted to see us already at the pool ready to start when he gets there.

two thirds of the way there and the stories of the weekend and laughter filled the air when we noticed on the other side of of the rugby field Mr Gumunyu bolting towards the swimming pool gates.
